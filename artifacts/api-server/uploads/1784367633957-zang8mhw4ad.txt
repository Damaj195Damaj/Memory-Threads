Dear Dr. Wren,
This letter confirms that the Ministry of Culture and Antiquities grants the University of Caldbridge,
under your direction as Principal Investigator, authorization to conduct archaeological survey and
excavation at the site known as Kestrel Hollow, Ardenmoor Highlands, for the period of 2 June
2025 through 9 June 2025 inclusive, in connection with grant AHT-2025-114 sponsored by the
Ardenmoor Heritage Trust.
This authorization is granted subject to the following conditions:
1. All excavation activity shall remain within the coordinates submitted in the accompanying site
plan.
2. Any artifact of significant historical, religious, or monetary value recovered during the permitted
period shall be reported to this office within 48 hours of recovery and transferred under seal to an
accredited conservation facility; removal from the country of origin is not authorized under this
permit.
3. No excavation activity by parties other than those named on this permit is authorized at the site
during the permitted period. Unauthorized survey, removal, or interference by third parties should
be reported immediately to this office.
4. A full written report of findings shall be submitted to this office within thirty (30) days of the close
of the permitted period.
5. This permit does not authorize excavation beyond 9 June 2025 without a separate extension
request.
Please contact this office directly should any circumstance arise affecting the security of the site or
any recovered materials during the permitted period.
Sincerely,
Y. Okafor
